Understanding Researcher Experiences of Recruitment

Within Digital Clinical Trials for Psychosis

More About This Study

Digital interventions for psychosis are increasingly available. Little is known about the willingness of people diagnosed with schizophrenia to be recruited into digital intervention clinical trials or what factors may influence this (Lim & Penn, 2018). Using a Delphi study, this research aims to recruit trial staff who are working (or have worked) in clinical trials of digital interventions for psychosis to identify and reach agreement on what factors are believed to influence recruitment.
